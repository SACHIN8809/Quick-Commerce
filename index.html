A Study to understand Strategic Determinants which influence Consumer's Behaviour in Quick Commerce Industry.

Hello,
Greetings from IMI New Delhi!

We are conducting a research to understand the strategic levers which influence consumer's behaviour in the Quick Commerce setting (Fast delivery service platforms). The objective this study is to systematically evaluate how service exchange, resource integration, institutional alignment, experiential value, and recovery mechanisms shape long-term customer engagement and retention within rapid-delivery ecosystems.

As part of this effort, we are undertaking a structured Multi-Criterion Decision Making (MCDM) expert prioritization exercise to identify which themes and sub-themes are most strategically critical for:
  • Managers seeking sustainable market capitalization and competitive advantage in the Quick Commerce sector, and
  • Consumers, aiming to achieve efficient value realization and platform fit.
Given your expertise and domain insight, we would greatly value your assessment of the relative importance of these constructs. Your inputs will help us understand the context more from industry viewpoint and we are hopeful that these will directly inform the theoretical advancements and managerial relevance of this study

This instrument is concise and designed to require approximately 10 minutes of your time. .
